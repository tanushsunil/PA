When a customer stops doing business with the company the customer is churned.The businesses are keen on keeping the existing customer because it is far less resource consuming than acquiring a new customer. Existing customers will often have a higher volume of service consumption and can generate additional customer referrals. Customer churn can be resolved by giving a good product and services.Preventing customer churn is critically important in the telecommunication sector in retail as barriers to entry switching services and providers are so low.
